## Some Interesting Parametric Curves

Butterfly:
$$
\begin{cases}
	x = sin(t)(e^{cos(t)} - 2 cos(4t) - sin^5(t/12)) \\
	y = cos(t)(e^{cos(t)} - 2 cos(4t) - sin^5(t/12)) \\
\end{cases}
$$
$0\leq t\leq 12\pi$

Archimedes' Spiral:
$$
\begin{cases}
	x =  (vt+c) cos(\omega t) \\
	y = (vt+c) sin(\omega t) \\
\end{cases}
$$
